# Warranty and Support

## Warranty Coverage
All products carry a 24-month (2-year) manufacturer warranty against factory
defects. The warranty covers repair or replacement at no cost, but does not
cover accidental damage, misuse, or normal wear and tear.

## How to Claim a Warranty
To claim a warranty, contact support with your order number and a description
of the issue. Approved claims are processed within 7 business days.

## Contacting Support

+1-800-123-4567.

## Support Hours
Our support team is available Monday to Friday, from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
Requests received outside these hours are answered on the next business day.
