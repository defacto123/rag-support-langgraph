# Service Agreement

## Article 1 — Scope
The Provider agrees to deliver consulting services to the Client as described
in the accompanying statement of work.

## Article 2 — Cancellation Period
The Client has the right to cancel this agreement within 14 days of signing,
with no penalty. Cancellations after this period are subject to the terms in
Article 4.

## Article 3 — Price
The price of the service is $1,000, including all applicable taxes. Payment is
due within 30 days of the invoice date.

## Article 4 — Late Cancellation
If the Client cancels after the 14-day window, a cancellation fee equal to 25%
of the total price applies.
